My Journey Into The World Of Coding Began
About 3 Hours Ago
In 2021, my journey into the world of coding began. I delved into the vast realm of web development, seeking knowledge from diverse sources such as websites and YouTube. As an avid reader, I found my primary learning path illuminated by the insights shared in documentaries. The enchanting world of front-end development unfolded before me through resources like FreeCodeCamp, MDN, and JavaScript.info. Navigating this learning adventure, I stumbled upon invaluable guidance from remarkable individuals and platforms such as Dorian Develops, Hitesh, and Codevolution. The journey continues, and I am eager to explore more, build, and contribute to the dynamic world of web development.
